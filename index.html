Auto-map generator

Builds a chart from the song's beats. Needs audio loaded — it auto-detects the beats & BPM if needed.

Tip: re-run to re-roll the patterns. Generation is one undo step.

Editing

  • Click a lane to place / remove a note
  • Click + drag vertically to draw a hold note
  • Drag a hold's end to scale its length
  • Grab the middle of a note to move it
  • Shift + drag on empty space to box-select
  • M mirror · Delete remove · Ctrl+C/V copy/paste

Navigation

  • Space play / pause
  • ↑ / ↓ or scroll move by one snap
  • Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y undo / redo
  • Ctrl+S save the mapset (.osz)
  • 1–9 place a note in that column at the playhead

Test play

  • ▶ Test launches immersive play from the playhead
  • Default keys auto-map per column (e.g. 4K = D F J K)
  • P pause · R retry · Esc back to the editor

Difficulties

  • Setup tab → Add / Duplicate / switch / delete
  • All diffs share the song; .osz exports them together
